What is Blockbiva Pro?

Blockbiva Pro is a next-generation Full Site Editing (FSE) WordPress theme built for startups, SaaS platforms, and agency websites. It features a complete range of block templates and design options.

Do I need a page builder?

No! Blockbiva is a pure block theme that works entirely with the native Gutenberg WordPress Editor, allowing you to edit all sections, headers, and footers directly without coding.

Can I cancel my plan anytime?

Yes, absolutely. There are no contracts, and you can cancel or change your plan at any time directly from your account settings.
